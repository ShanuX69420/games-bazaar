GamesBazaar Community Rules

1. Basic Rules for Everyone

What You Can't Do What Happens If You Break This Rule
1.1. Don't share your phone number, Discord, Telegram, or social media with other users. Don't use contact info they give you. Don't message them in games after your order is done. First time: Temporary account block. Keep doing it: Permanent account block and you lose your money.
1.2. Don't write fake reviews, threaten people for reviews, or change old reviews for no good reason. We delete fake reviews. Keep doing it: Account gets blocked (but you keep your money).
1.3. Don't share someone else's personal info (like their username, player ID, or payment details) to hurt them. All your accounts get blocked forever and you lose your money.
1.4. Don't try to buy or sell GamesBazaar accounts. All your accounts get blocked forever (but you keep your money).
1.5. Don't put inappropriate pictures, website links, or political stuff in your profile picture. We remove your profile picture. Keep doing it: Account gets blocked (but you keep your money).
1.6. Don't put website links or political stuff in your username. We change your username for you.
1.7. Don't spam the chat, advertise other websites, insult people, or post political stuff. Don't criticize other sellers publicly. You can't use chat for a while. Keep doing it: Account gets blocked (but you keep your money).
1.8. Don't insult, threaten, or spam people in private messages. Don't send inappropriate content without permission. First: Warning. Keep doing it: Account gets blocked temporarily.
1.9. Don't spam or send mass messages to users. Account gets blocked temporarily or permanently (but you keep your money).
1.10. Don't scam or try to hurt other users on purpose. All your accounts get blocked forever and you lose your money.
1.11. Don't use GamesBazaar to exchange money between payment methods without buying anything. No credit card fraud or stolen money. All your accounts get blocked forever and you lose your money.
1.12. Don't share random file links unless it's actually needed (like sharing account login details through an image). First: Warning. Keep doing it: Account gets blocked (but you keep your money).

Important: Sometimes our system will let you join Discord voice chat for certain sales. But you still can't exchange personal contact info or add each other as friends - that's still against the rules.

If you want to work with another seller on orders together, contact our support team first.

Our staff might test sellers by pretending to be regular buyers to make sure everyone follows the rules.

We can reduce or remove punishments if we think they don't fit the situation.

2. Rules for Sellers

2.1. Things Sellers Can't Do

What You Can't Do What Happens If You Break This Rule
2.1.1. Don't try to sell or trade outside of GamesBazaar's payment system. All transactions must go through our platform. All your accounts get blocked forever and you lose your money.
2.1.2. Don't ask buyers to mark orders as complete before you actually finish the work (unless you think they're trying to scam you). Account gets blocked temporarily and no more instant withdrawals.
2.1.3. Don't buy from competitors just to leave them bad reviews or make fake complaints. Your main account gets blocked temporarily.
2.1.4. Don't ignore your customers' questions for no good reason. First: Warning. Keep doing it: Account gets blocked.
2.1.5. Don't post fake listings or wrong prices on purpose. Account gets blocked temporarily. Might become permanent (but you keep your money).
2.1.7. When using "Automatic delivery", only put actual products in the product field. Don't add extra random information. First: Warning. Keep doing it: Account gets blocked.
2.1.8. Don't use "Automatic delivery" for things that need communication or extra work (like account re-linking). First: Warning. Keep doing it: Account gets blocked.
2.1.9. Follow the posting rules for each section. Don't ignore messages from GamesBazaar staff. We delete all your listings and give a warning. Keep doing it: Account gets blocked temporarily.
2.1.10. Don't post the same listing multiple times. Don't post "buying" requests in selling sections. We delete all your listings. Keep doing it: Account gets blocked temporarily.
2.1.11. Post your items in the right category (don't sell game currency in the items section, etc.). We move it to the right section and charge the correct fee. Keep doing it: Account gets blocked (but you keep your money).
2.1.12. Don't use inappropriate images, people's photos, website links, or political content in your listings. We delete the images or listings. Keep doing it: Account gets blocked (but you keep your money).
2.1.13. Don't post anything that violates other people's rights or could get them in trouble. We delete your listings. Keep doing it: Account gets blocked (but you keep your money).

2.2. Things You Can't Sell

What You Can't Sell What Happens If You Try
2.2.1. Anything stolen, hacked, or obtained illegally (like hacked accounts, stolen credit cards, etc.) Account blocked forever. You need to verify your identity within 60 days to get your money.
2.2.2. Teaching people how to hack, steal, or do other illegal things. Account blocked forever. You need to verify your identity within 60 days to get your money.
2.2.3. Personal information about other people. Account blocked forever. You need to verify your identity within 60 days to get your money.
2.2.4. Pirated software, viruses, or harmful programs. Account blocked forever. You need to verify your identity within 30 days to get your money.
2.2.5. Social media accounts or streaming service subscriptions (unless we have a special section for them). Account blocked forever. You need to verify your identity within 30 days to get your money.
2.2.6. Phone numbers (real or fake ones). Account blocked forever. You need to verify your identity within 30 days to get your money.
2.2.7. Lots of accounts at once (unless you created them all yourself). Account blocked forever. You need to verify your identity within 30 days to get your money.
2.2.8. Adult content or inappropriate material. Account blocked forever. You need to verify your identity within 30 days to get your money.
2.2.9. Spam services or mass messaging tools. Account blocked forever. You need to verify your identity within 30 days to get your money.
2.2.10. Gambling or casino related stuff. Account blocked forever. You need to verify your identity within 30 days to get your money.
2.2.11. Methods to artificially boost or promote accounts. Account blocked forever. You need to verify your identity within 30 days to get your money.
2.2.12. Shared game accounts or Game Pass subscriptions you're reselling. First: Warning. Keep doing it: Account gets blocked temporarily.
2.2.13. Lotteries, giveaways, or "mystery box" style random items. First: Warning. Keep doing it: Account gets blocked forever (but you keep your money).

Important: We can check any products listed on GamesBazaar to make sure they're legitimate.

3. When Sellers Have to Give Refunds

What Went Wrong How Much Money Back
3.1.1. You don't help solve disputes when GamesBazaar staff asks you to, or you ignore their messages. Buyer gets up to 100% of their money back.
3.1.2. You don't help your buyer or ignore their questions about their order. Buyer gets up to 100% of their money back.

3.2. Game Currency and Items

What Went Wrong How Much Money Back
3.2.1. The buyer got banned because you sold them stolen/hacked currency or items. Buyer gets up to 100% of their money back.
3.2.2. The buyer got banned just because they bought currency or items (not because it was stolen). Buyer gets up to 50% of their money back.
3.2.3. You didn't cover the game's transfer fees, so the buyer got less than they paid for. You pay back the missing amount.
3.2.4. You included bonus currency in your advertised amount, which isn't allowed. You pay back the value of the bonus currency.

3.3. Game Accounts

What Went Wrong How Much Money Back
3.3.1. You or the original owner took the account back from the buyer. Buyer gets up to 100% of their money back.
3.3.2. The account got banned just because it was bought/sold. Buyer gets up to 50% of their money back.

3.4. Services (Like Boosting)

What Went Wrong How Much Money Back
3.4.1. You changed something important on the account without asking (like losing items). You pay for the damage you caused.
3.4.2. You made things worse instead of better (like lowering rank instead of raising it). Full refund plus you pay for the damage.
3.4.3. The account got banned because you used bots or cheats while doing the service. Full refund plus extra compensation.
3.4.4. The account got banned because of the service you provided. Buyer gets up to 50% of their money back.
3.4.5. You refuse to finish the work for no good reason. You only get paid for 50% of what you actually completed.
3.4.6. You're late finishing the work. Your payment gets reduced based on how late you are. If you're 2x late, you get 50% payment.

Remember: Every dispute is different, so we might decide on different refund amounts than what's listed above. We'll also consider any special agreements you made with the buyer.

Need Help?

If you have questions about these rules or need help, contact our support team through the Support Center.
